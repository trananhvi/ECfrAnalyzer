Understanding the Metrics
Regulatory Complexity Index (RCI)

A custom metric that combines three factors:

  • Verbosity: Average words per regulation (40% weight)
  • Scope: Number of unique CFR titles covered (30% weight)
  • Structure: Complexity based on parts, chapters, and sections (30% weight)

Higher RCI values suggest agencies with more complex regulatory frameworks that may benefit from streamlining efforts.

Data Integrity

Each agency has a unique checksum that changes when regulations are updated, helping track changes over time.

Deregulation Insights

Agencies with high word counts, many regulations, or high complexity scores represent potential targets for deregulation initiatives to reduce administrative burden.
